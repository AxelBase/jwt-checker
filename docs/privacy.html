Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 8, 2025

At AxelBase, your privacy is not a feature — it’s the foundation. The JWT Checker is a zero-knowledge web tool that processes all data exclusively in your browser. We do not collect, store, transmit, or log any information — ever.

1. What We Do NOT Collect

This tool is engineered for absolute data isolation. We never receive:

  • JWT Tokens: Any token you paste is decoded using atob() and JSON.parse() — entirely on your device.
  • Personal Data: No names, emails, IP addresses, or device identifiers.
  • Usage Analytics: No Google Analytics, no cookies, no pixels, no beacons.
  • Network Requests: Open DevTools → Network tab → paste a token → zero outgoing traffic.

2. How the Tool Works (Client-Side Only)

When you use the JWT Checker:

  • Input: You paste a JWT into a <textarea>.
  • Processing: JavaScript splits the token, base64-decodes the payload, and parses iat and exp — all in memory.
  • Output: Results are rendered directly in the DOM. No fetch(), no WebSocket, no storage.
  • Export: “Copy Re-encoded JWT” uses navigator.clipboard — still local.

Your browser is the server.

3. No Third-Party Services

We do not integrate with:

  • Analytics platforms
  • Advertising networks
  • CDNs with tracking
  • Social media widgets

All assets (CSS, JS, fonts) are either self-hosted or loaded from privacy-respecting CDNs.

4. Cookies & Local Storage

No cookies. No localStorage persistence. Your input is cleared on page reload or when you click “Clear”.

5. Security Responsibility

While we don’t handle your data, we recommend:

  • Use the tool in a secure browser (HTTPS only)
  • Avoid pasting production tokens in public environments
  • Keep your OS and browser updated

6. Children's Privacy

This tool is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly process data from minors.

7. Global Compliance

Because no data is collected, we comply by default with:

  • GDPR (EU)
  • CCPA/CPRA (California)
  • LGPD (Brazil)
  • PIPEDA (Canada)

8. Updates to This Policy

We may revise this policy. Changes will be posted here with the updated effective date.

9. Contact

Questions? Reach out via our GitHub repository.

Your tokens. Your browser. Your privacy. Guaranteed.
